GOLD
Once European explorers “discovered” the Americas, they were eager to get their hands on American gold and silver. 



GLORY
"Glory" refers to the desire for national prestige and personal fame. 



GOD
Initially, Europeans were motivated by a desire to convert Muslims and Buddhists.
Later, this missionary impulse was extended to the peoples of the Americas.
Missionary zeal was intensified by the Protestant Reformation.
